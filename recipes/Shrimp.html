Marinated Grilled Shrimp

A very simple and easy marinade that makes your shrimp so yummy you don't even need cocktail sauce! Don't let the cayenne peper scare you,you don't even taste it. My 2 year old and 4 year-olds love it and eat more shrimp than their parents! It is also a big hit with company,and easy to prepare. i make this with frozen or fresh shrimp and use my indoor electric grill if the weather is not good for outside grilling.try it with a salad,baked potato,and garlic bread. You will not be disappointed!!!

Ingredients

  • 3 clove garlic,miced
  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 1/3 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons red wie vinigar
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
  • 1/2teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 2 pound frech,peeled and deveined
  • 6 each skewers

Steps

    Step 1

  1. In a large bowl, stir togeather the garlic, olive oil, tomatosauce, and red wine vinigar. Season with basil, salt, and cayenne pepper. Add shrimp to the bowl, and stir until evenly coated.Cover, and refrigerate for 30 min to 1 hour, stirring once or twice.

    2. Step 2

  2. Preheat grill for medium heat. Tread shrimp onto skewers, piercing once near the tail and once near the head. Discard Marinade

    3. Step 3

  3. Lightly oli grill grate. Cook shrimp on preheated grill for 2 to 3 minutes per sid, or until opaque.
