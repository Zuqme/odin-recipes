Classic Waffles

A lovely,crispy waffle perfect for the morning.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups warm milk
  • 1/3 cup butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vinilla extract

Steps

    Step 1
  1. In a large bowl, mix together flour, salt, baking powder and sugar; set aside. Preheat waffle iron to desired temperature
    2. Step2
  2. In a seperate bowl, beat the eggs. Stir in the milk, buteerand vinilla. Pour the milk mixture into the flour mixture; beat until blended.
    3. Step 3
  3. Ladle the batter into a preheated waflle iron. Cook the waffles until golden and crisp. Serve immediatley
